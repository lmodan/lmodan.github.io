2 mille miglia

A journal on the roads of 猫大师 & 长颈鹿

    • 中文A Last Drop of the Sun

    10 November ’17

    小小老鼠

    Tuscany, Italy

    greeted us with the sub-optimal scenery of the year, some drought problems, nevertheless the landscape is still breathtakingly beautiful.

    We have had left Karlsruhe very early in the morning and hasted southwards as fast as possible to catch the first rays of sunlight right at the feet of those snow-covered summits, at least it was planned as such. In Switzerland traffic jammed along the Autobahn and Herr Giraffe has made a wrong decision to head for Lucerne while the navigation opted for Zurich, effectively prolonging the journey to Italy for another hour. Plus, as daylight falls there is no way the sun shows himself, not on this side of the Alps to say the least, and by the time we reached Lugano on the other side, it was already noon.

    Gladiator Road as per our agriturismo host, plus the wheat/weed Russel may have had caressed. A view of Agriturismo A Terrapille from NE near Pienza, Italy.

    Moon rising as sunset paints Cappella di Vitaleta rosepink. Shot from parking section on Strada Provinciale 146 east of Agriturismo Poderino. The tower of Abbazia di Sant'Antimo.
