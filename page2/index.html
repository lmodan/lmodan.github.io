    • The First Blog

    10 November ’17

    This is the first blog of our website.

    This website is compiled with Jekyll and hosted on Github. After hours experimenting with pagination and multilingual Herr Giraffe eventually surrendered and called it a day.

    Turning then for jekyll-paginate-v2 on the second attempt, with the locale settings, it is possible now to paginate/categorize with respect to different languages, the outcome becomes the current version of the website: operational but somehow not-so-elegant. Also theoretically it should work on tablets and smartphones, but some bits like the language selector may not adapt to specific screens so well.
